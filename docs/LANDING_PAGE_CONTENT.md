### Deep Dive 1: IMEI Tracking That Actually Works

**Headline:**

> Know exactly where every device is. Always.

**Body copy:**

> When you're moving hundreds of units a week, "we think that phone went to that order" isn't good enough.
>
> DeviceDock gives every device its own record the moment you add it — manually, via our bulk IMEI entry tool, or through an Excel upload. Each unit carries its own status that updates automatically as it moves through your workflow:
>
> - **In Stock** — available for sale
> - **Reserved** — committed to an active order
> - **Sold** — linked to a completed order with invoice
> - **Returned** — back in your warehouse, awaiting decision
> - **Damaged** — written off with a reason
>
> Every status change is logged with a timestamp and the user who made it. When a buyer disputes a shipment, you have the full record in three clicks.

**Feature callout chips:**

- Bulk IMEI entry (scan or type)
- Excel import with IMEI validation
- Per-unit activity log
- Manual sale by IMEI lookup
- Full order-to-IMEI linkage

## FAQ

**Section headline:**

> Questions? Answered.

---

**Q: What happens when my trial ends?**

> Your data is safe. You have 7 days of read-only access after the trial, so you can still export anything you need. If you subscribe, everything continues exactly where you left off. If not, your data is held for 60 days before being archived.

---

**Q: Can I change plans later?**

> Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. When you upgrade, you get access to the new features immediately. When you downgrade, you keep access until the end of your current billing period.

---

**Q: Is there a setup fee or onboarding charge?**

> No. DeviceDock has no setup fee, no mandatory onboarding session, and no hidden charges. Sign up, follow the in-app guide, and you're running.

---

**Q: How does the IMEI tracking work?**

> When you add inventory, you enter the IMEI or serial number for each individual unit. DeviceDock tracks the status of each unit (in stock, reserved, sold, returned, damaged) and links it to specific orders. You can also bulk-enter IMEIs via our entry tool or upload them via Excel.

---

**Q: How does DeviceDock handle HST?**

> You attach an HST/GST rate to each inventory purchase. When you create a sales order, you apply the appropriate provincial rate (13% Ontario HST, 5% Alberta GST, etc.). The HST Reconciliation page gives you a full breakdown of Input Tax Credits (HST you paid on purchases) vs. HST collected from sales, and your net CRA remittance position.

---

**Q: Can my accountant access the system?**

> Yes. Invite them with the Analyst role — they get full read-only access to reports and the HST reconciliation page, but cannot modify inventory or orders. You can also export reports as CSV for offline review.

---

**Q: We're a team of 3 but might grow. Which plan should we start with?**

> Start with Growth. It gives 10 users (plenty of room to grow), unlimited inventory and orders, and full HST reconciliation — which you'll need as your volume increases. Starter is limited to 200 orders/month, which can become a constraint faster than most teams expect.

---

**Q: Do you charge in Canadian dollars?**

> Yes. All plans are priced and billed in CAD. Your subscription invoices are denominated in CAD and will include your applicable GST/HST if you provide your business address during signup.

---

**Q: Is my data secure?**

> Your company's data is completely isolated from other companies on the platform. DeviceDock uses row-level security at the database level — meaning even if two companies are on the same infrastructure, neither can ever see the other's data. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

---

**Q: What if I need more users than my plan allows?**

> You can add extra users at CAD $15/user/month on Starter and Growth plans, without needing to upgrade your entire plan. If you find yourself adding many users regularly, upgrading to the next tier usually works out cheaper.
